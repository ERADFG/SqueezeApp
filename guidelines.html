Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Anonymous doesn't mean unmoderated. These rules apply to every post and reply on InteractInk.

Not allowed

  • Illegal content of any kind, including content involving minors
  • Harassment, threats, or targeted abuse of any individual
  • Doxxing — sharing someone's private information without consent
  • Spam, scams, or repeated promotional posting
  • Malware, phishing links, or deceptive downloads
  • Content that infringes someone else's copyright
  • Coordinated brigading or manipulation of the board

How moderation works

We rely primarily on user reports. Every post has a Report link — use it to flag anything that breaks these rules. Reports are reviewed and, where warranted, the post is removed and repeat or severe violations result in an IP-level block.

What happens after a report

We review the reported post against these guidelines. If it violates them, it's removed. If we believe the content may be illegal, we may preserve relevant technical logs and, where legally required, share them with law enforcement.

Appeals

If you believe a post was removed in error, email michaelfuacci@gmail.com with the post ID and a brief explanation.
