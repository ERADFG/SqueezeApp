Community Guidelines

Anonymous doesn't mean unmoderated. These guidelines explain what keeps InteractInk usable for everyone.

Not allowed

  • Illegal content of any kind, including content exploiting minors
  • Harassment, threats, or targeted abuse of another person
  • Doxxing — posting someone's private information without consent
  • Spam, scams, or automated/bulk posting
  • Malware links or phishing attempts

How enforcement works

Posts that break these guidelines are removed when reported or detected. Repeat violations can result in a network-level block. See the FAQ for how to report a post.

Appeals

If you believe a post was removed in error, reach out via the Contact page with the post ID and a brief explanation.
