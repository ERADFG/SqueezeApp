About Squeezes

Welcome to Squeezes. We believe that professional-grade image optimization should be fast, free, and—most importantly—private.

In an era where most "online" tools require you to upload your personal or work-related files to a remote server, we decided to take a different approach. Squeezes is built using advanced browser-based technology, allowing us to perform high-quality image compression directly on your local device.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide developers, designers, and everyday users with a streamlined, secure way to manage file sizes without ever compromising data privacy. Your files never touch our servers, and they never leave your computer, ensuring that your data stays exactly where it belongs: with you.

Built for Speed

By removing the need for server uploads, we eliminate waiting times. Whether you are shrinking a single profile picture or processing a bulk batch of assets for a project, Squeezes is designed to handle your workflow in milliseconds.

Thank you for choosing Squeezes. We are constantly improving our algorithms to ensure the best possible balance between compression and quality.
