About InteractInk

InteractInk is a text board where you can post and reply without creating an account. There's no username, no profile, and no sign-up form. You write something, it goes up, and other people can reply to it.

Why anonymous?

A lot of discussion boards ask you to build a profile before you can say anything. We skip that step. You don't need an email address, a password, or a history tied to your name. What you post is judged on its own, not on who posted it.

How replies work

When you leave a reply, the site generates a short ID for you within that thread — something like Anon#a1b2. This lets people follow a back-and-forth conversation without needing your real identity. The ID only applies to that thread; it's not a persistent account.

Who runs this

InteractInk is built and maintained by an independent developer based in Uzbekistan. It's a small, independently run project — not a company product. If you want to reach out, see the Contact page.

Moderation

Anonymous doesn't mean unmoderated. Posts that break our Community Guidelines get removed, and repeat abuse gets blocked at the network level. See our Guidelines and Terms of Service for specifics, and use the Report link on any post to flag something.

What we're working on

The board is actively maintained. If you run into a bug or have a feature request, the Contact page has the fastest way to reach us.
