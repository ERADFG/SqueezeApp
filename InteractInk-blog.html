How to Use InteractInk Safely

InteractInk is designed to be simple, but a few habits will make your experience better and keep the board usable for everyone.

Posting

You don't need an account, but you're still responsible for what you post. Keep it within the Community Guidelines — no illegal content, harassment, doxxing, or spam.

Replying and tripcodes

When you reply, InteractInk generates a short tripcode so others can follow the conversation. Use >> followed by a post ID to link directly to the post you're responding to — it keeps threads readable as they grow.

Protecting your own privacy

InteractInk doesn't ask for personal information, but what you choose to type is up to you. Avoid including identifying details you wouldn't want public, and see our privacy basics guide for more.

Reporting content

If you see something that breaks the guidelines, use the Report link on that specific post. Reports are reviewed and acted on faster than general email.

Getting help

For anything else — bugs, feature ideas, or questions — check the FAQ first, then reach out via the Contact page.
