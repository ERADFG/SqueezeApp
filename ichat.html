How to Start a Secure Chat

Because ichat operates entirely serverless, devices need a direct 2-step handshake to discover each other's local network coordinates.

  1. Host Generates Offer: Click Invite Link to build a secure WebRTC invitation and copy it to your clipboard. Deliver it to your peer securely.
  2. Peer Generates Answer: Your partner simply opens that invitation link in their browser to automatically generate a Return Token.
  3. Complete Tunnel: Copy your partner's Return Token, paste it into the Establish Tunnel input field on your device, and click Complete Tunnel.

Once processing finishes, the session is fully active.
