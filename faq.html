Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data uploaded to your server?

No. Squeezes uses local browser-based technology. Your images are processed entirely on your device. We do not have access to your files, and they never leave your computer.

What file formats are supported?

Currently, we support most common image formats including JPEG, PNG, and WebP. You can convert between these formats easily using the selector in the control panel.

Why is my image quality different?

We use a smart compression algorithm that prioritizes visual fidelity. While the file size is reduced, we aim to maintain the original look of the image. You can test different settings if you need even smaller file sizes.

Is Squeezes really free?

Yes! Squeezes is a free tool. We use non-intrusive advertisements to help cover the costs of hosting and development, allowing us to keep this service free for everyone.

How do I contact support?

If you encounter any bugs or have feature requests, please reach out to us through our social media channels or the contact information provided in our main portal. We value your feedback!
