Frequently Asked Questions

Is my personal identity recorded?

No. InteractInk is completely anonymous. There are no registration forms, profile creations, or static user account databases. Your posts and replies exist independently on the network timeline layer without being tied to real-world metadata.

How do replies track authorship without usernames?

When you comment within a post container, your browser uses a locally generated private seed key to construct a secure cryptographic tripcode string (e.g., Anon#a1b2). This lets other nodes track identity continuity inside that specific thread block while preserving absolute anonymity.

What are the reference anchors (>>id)?

Every post and reply generates a unique 8-character matrix node ID. By typing ">>" followed by a target ID in a comment, you create an internal node cross-reference link. Hovering over or clicking these anchors surfaces a live context preview of the referenced update block.

Is InteractInk really free?

Yes. InteractInk is fully open, free to use, and requires zero subscription tiers. We utilize lightweight, privacy-conscious advertisement implementations to help fund decentralized hosting environments and infrastructure stability.

How do I report bugs or submit ideas?

We are constantly refining the sync protocols, layout rendering engine, and pagination features. If you spot a network disruption or want to request a feature, please reach out via our official developer support networks listed inside our community portals.
