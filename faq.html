Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account?

No. There's no sign-up, no login, and no profile. You can post and reply right away.

Is my identity recorded?

We don't ask for your name, email, or any personal details to post. We do log basic technical data (like IP address) for abuse prevention and legal compliance — see our Privacy Policy for exactly what's collected and how long it's kept.

What's the "Anon#a1b2" tag under my replies?

That's a short ID generated automatically when you reply in a thread. It lets other people follow a conversation between two or more anonymous posters without anyone needing a username. It only applies within that specific thread — it's not a persistent identity or account.

What do the >>12345678 links do?

Every post gets a unique ID. Typing >> followed by that ID creates a link back to the post you're replying to, so readers can see exactly what a reply is responding to. Hovering over the link shows a quick preview.

Is InteractInk free?

Yes, completely. We run a small amount of advertising to cover hosting costs. We don't charge for posting, replying, or reading.

What content isn't allowed?

Illegal content, harassment, doxxing, spam, and a few other categories are not allowed. The full list is in our Community Guidelines.

How do I report a post?

Every post has a Report link. Click it and tell us what's wrong — we review reports and remove content that breaks our guidelines. You can also email us directly; see the Contact page.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Email us — details are on the Contact page. We read everything, even if we can't reply to each message individually.
