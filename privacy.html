Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Squeezes, we believe your data belongs to you. We have built our image compression tool with a "Privacy-First" philosophy, meaning we do not store, track, or share your personal files.

How We Handle Your Data

Local Processing: When you use Squeezes, your image files are processed entirely within your web browser (locally on your device). This means your photos, documents, and images are never uploaded to a remote server or stored in a database.

Zero Data Retention: Because no files are sent over the internet for processing, Squeezes has no access to your content. Once you close the tab or refresh your browser, any temporary data used for the compression process is cleared from your device's memory.

Third-Party Services

While your image data remains private, we use standard analytics (like Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site so we can improve the experience. Additionally, we may display advertisements to help cover the costs of keeping this tool free. These ads are served by third-party providers who may use cookies to show relevant content based on your browsing habits.

Your Consent

By using Squeezes, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have any questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please feel free to reach out to us.
